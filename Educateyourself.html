Anxiety
Anxiety is a common mental health condition caused by excessive worry, fear. It can manifest through physical symptoms like increased heart rate, restlessness ,etc, as well as psychological symptoms like constant worry or difficulty concentrating.
Anger
Anger is a natural emotional response to perceived threats or frustrations, often characterized by feelings of irritation, rage, and hostility. It can lead to physical reactions such as increased heart rate, tension, and agitation.
Sadness
Sadness is a natural emotional response to loss, disappointment, or other challenging experiences, often marked by feelings of sorrow, heaviness, and low energy. It can affect one's overall mood and outlook on life.
Stress
Stress is the body's reaction to challenging situations, marked by feelings of tension, anxiety, and overwhelm. It can impact both mental and physical health, leading to symptoms like headaches, fatigue, and irritability.
Loneliness
Loneliness is the feeling of being isolated from others, even when surrounded by people. It can lead to emotional distress and a sense of emptiness, affecting both mental and physical health. Prolonged loneliness can contribute to issues like anxiety.
Depressed
Depression is a mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a lack of interest in daily activities. It can affect one's thoughts, behavior, and physical health.
